Happy Holi ✨

Happy Holi to the one who brings color into my calm days.

Some colors don’t shout. They stay — and quietly make everything brighter.
Maybe this Holi brings something new.

Not something loud.
Not something rushed.

Just a little more understanding.
A little more courage.
A little more clarity.

And if nothing changes,
I’m still grateful for what already exists.
Whatever this becomes — I’m glad it’s you.
